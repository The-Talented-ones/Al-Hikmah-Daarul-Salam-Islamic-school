Primary School Overview

Our Primary School program is designed to provide a strong educational foundation for children in their formative years. We focus on holistic development, combining academic excellence with character building and Islamic values. Our curriculum includes core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies, alongside Islamic studies to nurture both the mind and spirit. We also offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including arts, sports, and community service, encouraging students to explore their interests and talents. Our dedicated teachers create a supportive and engaging learning environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential.
