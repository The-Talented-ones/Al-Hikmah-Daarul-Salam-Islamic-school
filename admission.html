Admission Process

Welcome to Al-Hikmah Daarus-salaam Islamic School! We are delighted that you are considering joining our community. Our admission process is designed to ensure that we find the best fit for both our students and their families. Please follow the steps below to apply for admission:

Step 1: Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to request information about our school, programs, and application process.

Step 2: Application

Complete the online application form available on our website. Ensure that all required documents are submitted.

Step 3: Interview

Schedule an interview with our admissions team. This allows us to get to know you and your child better.

Step 4: Assessment

Depending on the grade level, your child may be required to complete an assessment to evaluate their academic skills.

Admission Process

Frequently Asked Questions about our admission requirements and procedures

Admission for the 2025/2026 academic year opens on January 15th, 2025. The process is as follows:
  • January 15 - March 31: Online application submission
  • April 15 - April 30: Entrance examinations
  • May 15: Release of results
  • May 16 - June 15: Interview sessions
  • June 30: Final admission list published

Early applications are encouraged as spaces are limited.

The following documents are required for a complete application:
For All Applicants:
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • 4 recent passport photographs
  • Medical report from recognized hospital
Additional Requirements:
  • Previous school report cards (last 2 years)
  • Transfer certificate (where applicable)
  • Testimonial from previous school
  • Immunization records

We have specific age requirements to ensure students are placed in appropriate learning environments:

Our fee structure is designed to be transparent and affordable. Fees vary by class level and include tuition, textbooks, and basic extracurricular activities.

Fee Structure (Per Term):
  • Nursery & Kindergarten: ₦25,000 - ₦50,000
  • Primary School: ₦50,000 - ₦100,000
  • Junior Secondary: ₦120,000 - ₦150,000
  • Senior Secondary: ₦150,000 - ₦180,000
Payment Options:
  • Full payment at beginning of term (5% discount)
  • Two installment plan
  • Three installment plan (with administrative fee)
  • Bank transfer, POS, or cash payments accepted

Additional fees may apply for specialized programs, field trips, and optional extracurricular activities.

Yes, all prospective students are required to take an entrance examination. The content varies by class level:

Primary School (Basic 1-6):
  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Quantitative Reasoning
  • Verbal Reasoning
Secondary School (JSS1-SSS1):
  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science
  • Social Studies
  • Current Affairs
Preparation: Sample past questions are available at the school's admin office. The examination typically lasts 2 hours.

Yes, we offer several scholarship opportunities for exceptional students:

Available Scholarships:
  • Academic Excellence Scholarship: For students with outstanding entrance exam results (up to 50% tuition)
  • Merit Scholarship: For students maintaining excellent academic performance (renewable annually)
  • Sports & Arts Scholarship: For students with exceptional talent in sports or creative arts
  • Sibling Discount: 10% discount for the second child, 15% for third and subsequent children
Financial Aid:

We have a limited number of need-based financial aid packages for families facing genuine financial constraints. Applications for financial aid must be submitted separately with supporting documents.
