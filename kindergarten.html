Kindergarten Overview

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Assalamu Alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh,

Welcome to the Kindergarten program at Al-Hikmah Daarul-Salaam Islamic School! Our kindergarten is a vibrant, nurturing, and safe haven where your child's educational journey begins with love, curiosity, and the light of Iman (faith).

We believe that the early years are the most critical for shaping a child's character, intellect, and love for their Creator. Our program is carefully designed to provide a balanced foundation, seamlessly integrating a robust academic curriculum with the timeless values of Islam.

Here, your child will not only learn their ABCs and 123s but also the beautiful words of Allah and the teachings of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

Key Features of Our Kindergarten Program

  • Integrated Islamic Curriculum
  • Quranic Studies & Arabic Introduction
  • Play-Based Learning Environment
  • Developmentally Appropriate Activities
  • Focus on Islamic Values and Ethics
  • Small Class Sizes for Individual Attention
  • Safe and Nurturing Islamic Environment
