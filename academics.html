We make scholars out of Students

At Al-Hikmah Daarul-salam Islamic School, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Our Academic Programs

Nursery

Our Nursery program focuses on early childhood development, providing a safe and nurturing environment for our youngest learners.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program builds on the foundation laid in Nursery, introducing more structured learning while still emphasizing play and exploration.

Primary

Our Primary program offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes core subjects, Islamic studies, and extracurricular activities to foster well-rounded development.

JSS 1-3

Our Junior Secondary School program prepares students for higher education with a focus on critical thinking, problem solving, and leadership skills.

#
SS 1-3

Our Senior Secondary School program offers a rigorous curriculum designed to prepare students for university and future careers, with a strong emphasis on academic excellence and personal growth.
