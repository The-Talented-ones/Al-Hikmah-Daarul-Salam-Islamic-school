About Evergreen Secondary

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 1985, Evergreen Secondary School has been committed to providing quality education that fosters intellectual growth, character development, and lifelong learning.

Our vision is to create a community of learners who are prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world with confidence, compassion, and critical thinking skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Admission to Evergreen Secondary is based on academic records, teacher recommendations, and a personal interview. Specific requirements vary by grade level. Please visit our Admissions page for detailed information.

Yes, we offer a range of financial aid options and merit-based scholarships. Approximately 30% of our students receive some form of financial assistance. Applications for financial aid are considered separately from admission applications.

We offer over 40 clubs and activities including robotics, debate, theater, various sports teams, community service groups, and academic competitions. New clubs can be formed based on student interest.

Our Learning Support Center provides individualized assistance for students with learning differences. We offer accommodations, specialized instruction, and work closely with families to ensure each student's success.

Regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Extended care is available from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM for an additional fee. After-school activities typically run from 3:15 PM to 5:00 PM.
