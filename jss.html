Junior Secondary School Program

Empowering Young Minds for a Brighter Future

Junior Secondary School Overview

Our Junior Secondary School (JSS) program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in both academic and personal development. We focus on nurturing critical thinking, problem solving, and leadership skills, preparing our students for the challenges of higher education and beyond. Our curriculum is comprehensive, covering core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, alongside Islamic studies and moral education. We also emphasize extracurricular activities, encouraging students to explore their interests and talents in sports, arts, and community service. At Al-Hikmah Daarul-salam Islamic School, we are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where every student can thrive and reach their full potential.

Key Features of Our JSS Program

  • Comprehensive Curriculum
  • Experienced and Dedicated Faculty
  • Focus on Islamic Values and Ethics
  • Extracurricular Activities and Clubs
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities
  • Personalized Learning Approach
  • Regular Assessments and Feedback
